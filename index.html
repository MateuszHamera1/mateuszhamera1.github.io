About me

I am originally from Gdansk, Poland but I moved to the Netherlands in order to study Game Design at Hanze University in Groningen. This portfolio is a collection of selected works that are supposed to showcase the skills I acquired throughout the studies.

My skills

My main goal is to become a 3D artist that creates complex models for games. My current skills include:

  • 3D modeling
  • 2D Animation
  • 3D texturing
  • Art for games

Besides being an aspiring 3D artist I am also very passionate about 2D art and 2D animation for games.

My work so far

During my studies at Hanze I worked as a digital artist on multiple projects. Here are some of the chosen assets I have created so far.

LOW POLY

This was the first school project where I could put my 3D skills into practice. We had to create a VR skiing game and my task was to model and texture a penguin, ice pick and a flashlight. I didn't know much about 3d modelling yet and I had to learn along with making the models. I made those very basic low poly assets and thanks to that I learned the basics of Blender and Substance Painter. It was a good point of entry to the world of 3D modelling as I could further improve my skills in the upcoming project.

MORE 3D

My next group project at Hanze is a Hyperloop VR simulation which has to portray what travelling with Hyperloop could look like in the near future. Agian, my task was to 3D model all of the needed assets. I used Blender and Substance Painter to make models that were a bit more detailed than those for the previous project. As I created more models, 3D modelling and texturing has slowly became much easier. During this project I learned some of the more advanced techniques and improved my workflow. Some of the models became interactive within the game.

MORE DETAILED 3D

The next step for me will be to learn how to create models that are much more realistic and detailed. So far I have made this gun for an individual art assigment at Hanze.

2D art and animation

I also made pixel art for 2D games. I had to make multiple sprites for various character animations and the enviroment surrounding them.
